Our Story

About Us

Leveling the playing field for every defence aspirant in India.

Welcome to the SSB Psych Test Platform—an educational initiative forged out of strict necessity, driven by intense passion, and dedicated entirely to the future officers of our armed forces.

We launched this platform after identifying a massive, systemic gap in how candidates prepare for Stage-2 of the Services Selection Board (SSB) interview. Every year, thousands of highly capable, physically fit, and intelligent candidates are screened out or conference-out. The ultimate reason? They simply freeze under the uncompromising time limits of the psychological test battery.

Our Core Mission

While premium coaching academies in metropolitan cities charge exorbitant fees to explain theoretical concepts, we realized that the actual core of the psychological test comes down to one fundamental truth: muscle memory and strict timed practice.

Our mission is straightforward and uncompromising: To provide every defence aspirant, whether they are sitting in a premium coaching institute or a remote village, with a realistic, high-quality, and completely free platform to practice PPDT, TAT, WAT, and SRT.

What Makes Us Different?

We aren't here to spoon-feed you "perfect" stories or "ideal" word associations. Psychology is highly subjective, and DIPR assessors are expertly trained to spot fake, pre-memorized responses. Instead of giving you the answers, we provide the ultimate environment.

  • Strict Timers: Our engine perfectly mimics the continuous, un-pausable pressure of the real exam hall.
  • Dynamic Content: With hundreds of images and words, no two mock tests are ever the same.
  • Notebook Philosophy: We force you to physically write with a pen and paper, building actual exam stamina.

Built By Enthusiasts, For Aspirants

This platform is an independent passion project. It is actively maintained and continuously updated to ensure candidates always have access to fresh stimuli and rigorous practice environments.

We voluntarily cover the costs of server hosting, database management, and domain maintenance ourselves—with a little help from community donations and platform advertisements. We do this to ensure that the core testing engine will always remain 100% free for everyone. There are absolutely no hidden paywalls and no mandatory sign-ups.

To every aspirant reading this: Keep practicing hard, stay deeply honest with your psychological responses, and never give up on the dream.
