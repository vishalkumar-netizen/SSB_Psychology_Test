Privacy Policy

At the SSB Psychological Test Platform (accessible from www.ssbpsychtest.in ), the privacy of our visitors is one of our absolute main priorities. This Privacy Policy document explicitly outlines the types of information that is collected and recorded by our platform, and exactly how we use it to provide you with a free educational service.

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to reach out via our Contact Us page.

1. The Most Important Rule: Your Psychological Data

We do NOT collect, store, or transmit your personal test answers to any server.

We deeply respect your privacy, especially regarding sensitive psychological testing. When you take a test on our platform—whether it is a TAT story, a WAT sentence, or an SRT reaction—all that data remains strictly on your local device (in your browser's local memory).

When you click "Download Question Paper" at the end of the test, the PDF file is generated directly on your computer or smartphone's processor. We have absolutely no backend servers recording, reading, or evaluating your psychological responses, ensuring your privacy is 100% safeguarded.

2. Standard Log Files

Like almost all standard websites, our platform follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. This is a standard part of our hosting services' analytics.

The information collected by log files includes:

  • Internet Protocol (IP) addresses
  • Browser type and version
  • Internet Service Provider (ISP)
  • Date and time stamp
  • Referring/exit pages
  • Number of clicks

These logs are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable. The sole purpose of this information is for analyzing trends, administering the site, tracking users' movement on the website, and gathering broad demographic information.

3. Google AdSense and DoubleClick DART Cookie

To keep the core testing engine completely free for all aspirants, we use third-party advertising companies—specifically Google AdSense—to serve advertisements when you visit our website.

Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on our site. Specifically, Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to our users based on their previous visits to our site and other sites on the Internet.

Opting Out of DART Cookies:

Users may easily opt-out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the official Google Ad and Content Network privacy policy at the following URL:
https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

4. Advertising Partners & Third-Party Privacy Policies

These third-party ad servers or ad networks use technologies like cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons that are utilized in their respective advertisements and links that appear on our platform. These are sent directly to users' browsers, meaning they automatically receive your IP address when this occurs. These technologies are used to measure the effectiveness of their advertising campaigns and/or to personalize the advertising content that you see on websites that you visit.

Please note that the SSB Psychological Test Platform has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.

Our Privacy Policy does not apply to other advertisers or websites. Thus, we strongly advise you to consult the respective Privacy Policies of these third-party ad servers for more detailed information. It may include their practices and instructions about how to opt-out of certain options.

5. Children's Information

Another part of our priority is adding protection for children while using the internet. We encourage parents and guardians to observe, participate in, and monitor their online activity.

The SSB Psychological Test Platform does not knowingly collect any Personal Identifiable Information from children under the age of 13. If you think that your child provided this kind of information on our website, we strongly encourage you to contact us immediately, and we will make our best efforts to promptly remove such information from our records.

6. Your Consent

By using our website, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms and conditions.
