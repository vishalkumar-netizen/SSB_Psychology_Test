Welcome to the ultimate online preparation tool for the Services Selection Board (SSB) Interview. The psychological test battery is designed to evaluate a candidate's mental fortitude, emotional stability, and subconscious reactions. To succeed, you need practice under strict time constraints. Our test replicates the exact pressure of the actual testing hall.

🎯 What We Offer

  • TAT (Picture Perception) Write meaningful stories on sequential back-to-back pictures under strict time pressure.
  • WAT (Word Association Test) Test your subconscious by writing natural responses to words flashed for 15 seconds.
  • SRT (Situation Reaction Test) React to 60 everyday practical situations realistically within a 30-minute limit.
  • SDT (Self Description Test) Understand your personality by writing comprehensive self-assessments.

How to Use This Simulator

  • Ensure you are in a quiet environment free from distractions.
  • Have a physical notebook ready if you prefer writing, or use the on-screen typing interface provided below.
  • Select either a specific test from the Quick Actions above or the Full Mock Test panel below to begin. Note: Once a test begins, the timer cannot be paused.
  • Download Results: Upon completion, click the download button to generate a clean PDF Performance Report of your responses. You can easily save this PDF for self-analysis or to share with mentors.

About Us
